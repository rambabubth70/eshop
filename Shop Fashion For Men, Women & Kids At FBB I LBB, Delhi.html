    What Makes It Awesome

    FBB, in case you haven’t heard of it already, is the fashion leg of Big Bazaar. It offers affordable, daily wear clothes for men, women and children. We love that they’ve got stuff that’s in line with the on-going trends, and most of their stuff falls within INR 200 to INR 1,000 (very few pieces go beyond that). They’ve got multiple stores across town, but you can shop online as well, your first delivery will be free. 

    What Could Be Better

    While they’re great for casual daily wear, we’re not the biggest fan of their party wear (but that could just be a matter of personal taste).
